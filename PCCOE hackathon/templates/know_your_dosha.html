Understand Your Constitution

Explore the three fundamental energies that govern your body and mind.

Vata

Air + Ether

Creative, energetic, quick learner. Light frame, dry skin, sensitive digestion. Prone to anxiety and cold.

Dry skin, bloating, constipation, insomnia, anxiety, restlessness, joint pain.

Favor: Warm, oily, cooked foods. Sweet, sour, salty tastes.
Avoid: Cold, dry, raw foods. Pungent, bitter, astringent tastes.
Routine: Regular sleep, warm oil massage, gentle yoga.

Pitta

Fire + Water

Intelligent, focused, strong digestion. Medium build, warm skin. Prone to anger and overheating.

Acid reflux, skin rashes, inflammation, anger, irritability, excessive heat.

Favor: Cooling, raw, sweet foods. Sweet, bitter, astringent tastes.
Avoid: Hot, spicy, fried foods. Pungent, sour, salty tastes.
Routine: Stay cool, avoid midday sun, swimming, meditation.

Kapha

Earth + Water

Calm, loyal, strong endurance. Heavy build, smooth skin. Prone to lethargy and weight gain.

Weight gain, congestion, lethargy, depression, excessive sleep, water retention.

Favor: Light, dry, warm foods. Pungent, bitter, astringent tastes.
Avoid: Heavy, oily, sweet foods. Sweet, sour, salty tastes.
Routine: Wake up early, vigorous exercise, variety in routine.
